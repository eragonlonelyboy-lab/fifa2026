Team strength = World-Football Elo with Dixon-Coles exponential time-decay (913 internationals, Oct 2023 to Jun 2026, frozen pre-tournament), blended 69/31 with a squad market-value rating (Transfermarkt-style, log-scaled to the Elo axis) so current squad quality counts, not just past results. Each match: expected goals → Karlis-Ntzoufras bivariate Poisson (λ₃=0.10) with a Dixon-Coles low-score correction (ρ=−0.05), ensembled 90/10 with a Maher attack/defence Poisson. A tournament draw-rate calibration nudges the draw probability toward this World Cup's actual draw frequency (shrunk by sample size), applied to upcoming matches and the Monte Carlo only, never the frozen scorecard. An in-tournament form layer nudges each team's upcoming predictions by their over/under-performance vs expectation so far (bounded mini-Elo, forward-looking only). Tournament: Monte Carlo.

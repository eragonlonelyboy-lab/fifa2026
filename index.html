Methodology. Team strength = World-Football Elo with Dixon-Coles exponential time-decay (913 internationals, Oct 2023 to Jun 2026, frozen pre-tournament), blended 73/27 with a squad market-value rating (Transfermarkt-style, log-scaled to the Elo axis) so current squad quality counts, not just past results. The squad weight auto-tunes on live results, shrunk to a 0.25 prior. Each match: expected goals → Karlis-Ntzoufras bivariate Poisson (λ₃=0.10) with a Dixon-Coles low-score correction (ρ=−0.05), ensembled 90/10 with a Maher attack/defence Poisson. Tournament: Monte Carlo. The Elo+ensemble core backtests walk-forward out-of-sample at RPS 0.171 / 62% / ECE 2.0% (beats the open-source baseline 0.175); the squad-value blend is evidence-based (Groll et al.) but forward-looking, not back-tested on historical squad values. Live results + bookmaker odds: ESPN / DraftKings. The model never sees the odds. Apollo's Oracle, built by Eragon. Champion odds use a simplified rating-seeded bracket (approximate).
